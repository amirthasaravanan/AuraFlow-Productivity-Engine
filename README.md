# AuraFlow Productivity Engine
## ✨ AuraFlow: An Aesthetic, Private Student Planner
AuraFlow is a minimalist, distraction-free digital sanctuary designed to help students bring calm and organization to their academic life. Built with a focus on privacy and user experience, AuraFlow combines daily planning, academic tracking, and mindful reflection into one beautiful "glassmorphism" interface.

## 📝 Important Notes
A dedicated space for quick capture and vital reminders.
Sometimes you need to jot something down that doesn't fit into a "task" or a "journal entry." The Important Notes section is a separate, focused page where you can store meeting points, quick references, or sudden inspirations. It keeps your main dashboard clutter-free while ensuring your most important information is always one click away.

# 🛠️ Tech Stack & Philosophy
## Frontend: 
- HTML5
- CSS3 (Tailwind CSS)
- Vanilla JavaScript.

## Privacy First: 
100% Offline-first. AuraFlow uses the browser's localStorage, meaning your data never leaves your device and no internet connection is required.

## Zero Subscriptions: 
No accounts, no fees—just a clean tool for student success.
